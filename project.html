Our Construction & Renovation Projects

At AGJ Construction, we're proud to showcase a diverse portfolio of home renovation and construction projects completed across Auckland. Our team of licensed builders specialises in residential remodeling, home renovations, new builds, repairs, and comprehensive home improvement solutions. Each project demonstrates our commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and understanding of Auckland's unique building requirements. From modest residential repairs to complete home renovations and new construction, we've helped hundreds of Kiwi families transform their properties into homes they love. Our hands-on approach ensures every renovation job and construction project receives the professional expertise and personalized attention it deserves, maintaining close contact with our clients throughout the entire building process to achieve results that exceed expectations.
