Welcome to AGJ Construction

Welcome to AGJ Construction, your premier choice for house renovation and new builds across the wider Auckland region. Our team of qualified and Auckland-based licensed builders is dedicated to transforming your home or project with the highest standards of craftsmanship, professionalism, and a Kiwi commitment to quality.

Our Mission

As a New Zealand-based company deeply connected to the Auckland community, we understand the unique requirements of building and renovating homes in this diverse city. Our mission is straightforward: transform your vision into a reality. We believe that your home should reflect your lifestyle and personality, and we work closely with you to ensure your unique needs and desires are met with precision. Whether you're building a new home in Auckland, planning a major renovation in your existing property, or undertaking minor upgrades, we are here to guide you, support you, and deliver exceptional results every time.

Our Commitment

At AGJ Construction, quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction are at the absolute core of everything we do. We use only the finest, locally sourced and quality-approved materials and employ the latest construction techniques to ensure your home or project stands the test of time against Auckland's unique environment. Our commitment to transparent communication and a collaborative approach means you are involved in every decision, ensuring the final result not only meets but exceeds your expectations, leaving you with a home you love and can be proud of, right here in Auckland.
